Fraction Fun

Instructions

After selecting a reward and a game mode, you will be presented with a list of fractions and some colored tiles. Click and drag tiles from the "Tile Box" into the "My Solution" box until your collection of tiles matches each of the fractions provided. Whatever reward you choose will get better each time you get a task correct. Try to get all 10 right for the best reward!

Note: You'll only have one chance at each problem per game, so be sure to double check before clicking "Next"!
